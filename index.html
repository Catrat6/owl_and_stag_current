Introduction

Owl & Stag is a Philly-based production company specializing in the creation and execution of brand films for companies looking to beautify their brand's image and reconnect with their customers in a more memorable, organic way.

We are creatives first, with a team of directors, producers, designers, and post-production artists committed to developing engaging, multi-platform content for brands, agencies, and influencers.

We eat, sleep, and breathe digital content. Combining that with a love of storytelling we create pieces that last; that your company can be proud of down the road.

Simply put, we help brands stand out among their competition. We will work with you to consistently create strong content, and make you feel like you are putting your best foot forward with your public facing image.

Team

Phil Kwarta / Founder & Creative Director

Phil Kwarta is our award winning director whose multidisciplinary skill set has led him through both the narrative and the ad world.


Phil’s ability to conceptualize ideas, direct talent, and stay cool under pressure makes him the ideal candidate for any and all undertakings for Owl & Stag. His knowledge of the entire production process makes him an asset every project we undertake and his skills as a creative director help guide our clients through the challenges of the ideation process with ease.

Jordan Chasten / Art Director

Jordan “Gordon” Shasteen is the glue of all of our productions. With a burgeoning skillset in photography and lighting, Jordan continues to impress, as his skills grow with our team. Jordan always catches what we may miss and has proven himself to be an invaluable asset to all of our productions.


Jordan’s keen eye and intoxicating energy make all of our sets a fun place to be because at the end of the day, this is the reason we do this; to make beautiful visuals and having a great time doing it.

Joseph Balestra / Director of Photography

Joseph Balestra is our Director of Photography and hails from our sister company, Anatome Studios. Joseph’s work as a photographer taught him the skills necessary to light any visual piece with an eye for lighting unlike anyone else’s. Joseph’s style is rooted in the play between light and shadows as he regularly tests the boundaries of what’s possible with lighting while creating beautiful imagery that encapsulates the human spirit.


We trust Joseph to bring the best practices in lighting and camera work to all of our pieces and our clients’ expectations are regularly surpassed with everything he creates for them.
